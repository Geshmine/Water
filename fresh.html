Availability of fresh water

Let us look the availability of water under the topics Total water present on Earth,Fresh water available and Accesible fresh water.

  1. Total water present on Earth
    • Ocean Water (saline) - 97%
    • Fresh Water - 3%
  1. Fresh water available
    • Ice caps and Glaciers (inaccesable due to location) - 79%
    • Groundwater (becomes muddy) - 20%
    • Accessible fresh water - 1%
  1. Accesible fresh water
    • Lakes - 52%
    • Soil moisture - 38%
    • Water vapour - 8%
    • Rivers - 1%
    • Water inside Living Organisms - 1%
